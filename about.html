Vinayka Wires & Cable is a leading manufacturer of high-grade electrical wiring solutions based in the heart of Delhi's industrial belt. With a steadfast commitment to safety, efficiency, and reliability, we deliver products that power residential, commercial, and industrial projects across India. From our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility at Jhilmil Industrial Area, Shahdara, every conductor is crafted with precision copper and tested to exceed national quality standards.

Our Journey

2014

Founded by Prakash Chand Lodha

Vinayka Wires & Cable was established with a simple mission — manufacture copper wires that Indian electricians and builders could trust. We started with a small production unit in Shahdara, Delhi.

2016

Expanded to FLFR & Specialty Cables

Expanded our production lines to include Flame Retardant Low Smoke (FLFR) house wires, meeting growing fire-safety requirements in building construction across Delhi NCR.

2018

Industrial-Scale Manufacturing

Upgraded to full industrial-scale production at our Jhilmil Industrial Area facility, enabling us to serve large contractors, government projects, and commercial builders with consistent supply.

2021

Extension Boards Product Line Launch

Introduced our premium ISI-marked extension board range with 10+ models — from home-use 4-socket boards to heavy-duty industrial spike guards, serving retail and wholesale markets.

2024

PAN-India Distribution Network

Established partnerships with logistics providers to deliver products reliably to every corner of India, with 1,000+ satisfied clients and growing year-on-year.
